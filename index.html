Rural & Plural: Participatory Digital Cartographies

This interactive map allows participants to add points around the world with images, audio, video and contextual information. Click anywhere on the map to place a new contribution and share a memory, evidence or story.

Entries are stored locally in the browser, so the map can be expanded gradually and later exported or shared.
